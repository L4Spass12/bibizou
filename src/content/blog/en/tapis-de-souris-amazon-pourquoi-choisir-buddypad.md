## The problem with mouse pads on Amazon

### Thousands of references, little transparency

**Amazon** is a sellers' aggregator. For **mouse pads**, that concretely means you often buy from a middleman reselling mass-produced generic products without their own quality control. The same reference can be sold by 12 different sellers under 12 different brand names, with the same product visual.

Result: impossible to know what you're really buying before receiving the package. Real thickness, cloth quality, rubber base density - none of that shows on a product page optimized for conversions, not information. If you want to understand [how to choose a quality mouse pad](/en/choisir-tapis-souris-2026/), the technical criteria make all the difference.
