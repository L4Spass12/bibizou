## Manga mouse pad: frequently asked questions

### Do anime mouse pad colors hold up over time?

Yes, thanks to the thermal sublimation printing technique used on all our **manga mouse pads**. Unlike transfer printing where ink sits on the surface, sublimation embeds pigments directly into the fabric fibers. The result resists repeated washing without flaking or fading.

### Can you wash an anime mouse pad?

Yes. Hand-wash it with lukewarm water and mild soap, scrub gently and rinse thoroughly. Air-dry flat, never in a washing machine or tumble dryer. A wash every 1 to 2 months is enough to maintain glide performance and color vibrancy.

### Are anime mouse pads suitable for competitive gaming?

Absolutely. Our **manga gaming mouse pads** offer exactly the same performance as a classic gaming pad: optimized tracking surface, non-slip base, stitched edges. The design doesn't affect technical qualities at all. You can play at your best while showing your passion for **anime**.
