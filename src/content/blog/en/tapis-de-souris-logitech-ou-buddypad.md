At BuddyPad, we don't claim to be perfect. But we believe in total transparency with our community. That's why we decided to put our own pads through a rigorous **mouse pad comparison test** against one of the historical market leaders: Logitech. For three months, our team used our BuddyPad pads daily as well as several Logitech models to give you an honest, no-spin **mouse pad review**.

## Our test protocol: honesty first

We tested our pads under the same conditions as the **Logitech G440** (hard surface) and G640 (cloth): intensive gaming, office work, graphic design, and even some photo retouching sessions. The goal: identify where we excel, but also where we can still progress in this **gaming mouse pad comparison**.

## Glide quality and accuracy: the heart of performance

![Gaming mouse on a mouse pad - glide quality and accuracy with blue and red lighting effects simulating performance.](/images/inline/tapis-de-souris-qualite-de-glisse-et-precision-pour-gaming.webp)

### Logitech: recognized excellence

Let's be honest: [Logitech](https://www.logitech.com/) doesn't dominate the market by accident. The G440 (hard surface) is fearsomely effective for fast movements. The G640 in cloth provides remarkable control. Perfect surface consistency, flawless integration with their ecosystem, impeccable finish. **Logitech limits:** very plain designs, premium pricing ($35-55), premature wear on some models (G440 in particular).

### BuddyPad: our approach

Our pads use a high-density cloth offering a balance between speed and control. **Our strengths:** unbeatable value-for-money, original manga/anime/gaming designs, hand-stitched edges, generous formats up to XXL, surface that holds up over time. **Areas for improvement:** about a week of break-in, some designs too pronounced for strict professional use.

## Durability: what three months taught us

The Logitech G440 shows visible wear signs after three months of intensive use: micro-scratches and slight warping. The G640 holds up better, with some lint on the surface. Our BuddyPad pads kept their original look. Stitched edges hold perfectly, and print quality stays intact despite several cleanings.

## Price and value: our owned positioning

Logitech positions its pads between $35 and $55. Our BuddyPad pads sit between $25 and $40 for equivalent or larger formats. We don't claim to be a luxury brand - we want to make quality accessible.

## Our honest verdict

**Pick Logitech if:** you already have a complete Logitech setup, you prioritize ultra-minimalist aesthetics, your budget isn't a constraint, or you're looking for an ultra-fast hard surface.

**Pick BuddyPad if:** you're chasing the best value-for-money, you want a durable product, you want to express your personality through your setup, and you're part of gaming or anime culture.

## FAQ - Logitech vs BuddyPad

Are Logitech pads better than BuddyPad?+

Logitech excels at pure glide and surface consistency, especially with the G440 hard for competitive FPS. BuddyPad surpasses Logitech on value-for-money ($25-40 vs $35-55), stitched-edge durability, design diversity and XXL formats. For 90% of users, BuddyPad offers more value.

How long does a Logitech pad last compared to BuddyPad?+

After 3 months of intensive testing, the Logitech G440 shows visible micro-scratches and slight warping. BuddyPad pads keep their original look thanks to hand-stitched edges. Estimated lifespan: 3-5 years for BuddyPad vs 2-3 years for similar Logitech models.

Can you use a BuddyPad pad with a Logitech mouse?+

Yes, perfectly. BuddyPad pads are compatible with all Logitech G gaming mice (G Pro, G502, G303...). The optical sensor works optimally on the BuddyPad micro-cloth surface. Exclusive brand/pad compatibility is a marketing myth.

Does Logitech offer XXL gaming pads?+

Logitech mainly offers the Desk Mat Studio Series (70x30 cm / 28x12 in) and the G840 (90x40 cm / 35x16 in). BuddyPad also offers XXL formats up to 90x40 cm, but with original manga and gaming designs that Logitech doesn't have in its plain catalog.

Is the BuddyPad pad machine-washable like a Logitech?+

Yes, BuddyPad cloth pads can be machine-washed at 30°C / 86°F on delicate cycle, exactly like Logitech cloth pads. Always let dry flat to preserve the non-slip base. A pad that returns to its original performance after every wash.
