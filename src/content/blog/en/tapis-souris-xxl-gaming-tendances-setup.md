## FAQ XXL mouse pads and gaming setups

### What size for an XXL gaming mouse pad?

The standard format is 90×40 cm (35×16 in), ideal to cover keyboard and mouse on a normal-width desk (120 to 160 cm / 47 to 63 in). For very large desks or ultrawide setups, some models reach 120×60 cm (47×24 in).

### Desk mat or XXL mouse pad: what's the difference?

Both refer to the same type of product. "Desk mat" is more used in office and remote-work contexts, while **XXL mouse pad** is the dominant expression in the gaming universe. In practice, same format, same use.

### How do I maintain a large gamer mouse pad?

Hand-washing with lukewarm water and mild soap is enough. Avoid the tumble dryer, which warps stitched edges and alters colors. Air-dry flat. Our [how to wash a cloth mouse pad](/en/laver-tapis-souris-tissu/) guide details every step.

### Which mouse pad to choose for an aesthetic gaming setup?

Pick a design that matches your color palette and the mood you want to create. Samurai, floral, dark anime, minimalist or kawaii: the key is that the **gaming desk mat** talks to the rest of your desk.
