## A soft, safe floor to explore

The **foam puzzle play mat** creates a comfortable and protected play space for your baby from the very first months. The tiles interlock easily and can be reconfigured to fit your living room or nursery.

## Features

- **Material**: High-density EPE foam — soft to the touch, shock-resistant
- **Interlocking tiles**: free layout to suit your space
- **Non-slip**: textured bottom prevents sliding
- **Easy to clean**: a quick wipe is enough
- **BPA-free**: tested and safe materials for babies

## Why parents love it

Babies spend their first months crawling, rolling, and sitting — all activities that need a soft, hygienic floor. This puzzle mat protects little knees and hands while clearly defining the play area.
