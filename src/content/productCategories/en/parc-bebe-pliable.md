## How to choose the right foldable baby playpen?

A good **foldable and modular baby playpen** needs to tick three boxes: **material safety**, **configuration flexibility**, and **everyday usability**.

### Modular or not: the real difference

A modular baby playpen consists of independent panels that clip together. You can:
- Choose the **shape** (rectangle, square, hexagon, L-shape to follow your walls)
- Adjust the **size** based on the space available
- **Add or remove** panels over time

A fixed playpen is simpler but far less versatile. For an apartment or a home where space varies from room to room, modular is unbeatable.

### Foldable: a big plus for storage

A foldable baby playpen folds flat in seconds. That's essential if:
- You have a small space and want to put the playpen away at night
- You travel regularly (vacations, grandparents)
- You use it outdoors occasionally (garden in summer, living room in winter)

### Safety: what to check

- **Materials certified BPA-, phthalate-, and formaldehyde-free** (EN71 standard or equivalent)
- **Panel locking**: a press-to-unlock system prevents baby from opening it alone
- **Floor stability**: suction cups or non-slip feet to avoid tipping
- **Panel height**: minimum 60 cm, ideally adjustable double-height for kids who pull up to stand

### With or without play accessories?

"PlaySet" bundles usually include basketball hoop, slide, activity panels. Convenient for starting out but often less sturdy than toys bought separately. For long-term use, a quality bare playpen + individually chosen toys is the better bet.
